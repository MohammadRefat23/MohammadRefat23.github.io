\section{Research Interests}

%%% Astro
\textbf{Keywords:} Computational astrophysics, Time-domain astronomy, Light-curve inversion, Stellar surface mapping, Brown dwarf and exoplanet atmospheres, Bayesian inference, Scientific computing

\vspace{0.3em}

\textbf{Executive Summary:}
I am a computational astrophysicist interested in developing statistical and computational methods for extracting physical insight from astronomical observations. My research combines time-series analysis, inverse problems, and Bayesian inference to study stellar surface features, brown dwarf and exoplanet atmospheres, and other time-domain phenomena. More broadly, I am interested in applying scientific computing and probabilistic modeling to understand complex astrophysical systems.
