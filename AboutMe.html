About Me

I am a rising senior at the City University of New York’s Graduate Center’s CUNY BA program in New York City. My current research is in brown dwarf atmospheres and I am primarily interested in galactic archaeology and exoplanets. I am a member of the AstroCom NYC fellowship and currently doing research with the BDNYC (Brown Dwarfs in New York City) group at the American Museum of Natural History.
